CEO - Joseph Culter

Joseph Culter is the CEO of OPIX Technology and oversees the company’s global operations and drives the overall agenda and direction of the company. He started his career in 2004, and was invited by Peter Fitzhugh Brown to work at Renaissance Technologies as a research scientist. He has more than a decade of experience in doing quantitative research for medallion fund, which is the most enigmatic and impressive hedge fund on Wall Street.  In year 2017, he founded OPIX Technology, believing that the integration of exceptional talent, advanced quantitative analytics and leading-edge technology would generate consistent, strong long-term performance.

He holds a Doctor of Philosophy in Mathematical Sciences from University of Bath and Bachelor of Science in Mathematics from Imperial College London.

CTO - Alex Rowayve

Alex Rowayve is CTO of OPIX Technology. He directs the software engineering and Infrastructure teams, oversees trading technology strategy and technical operations of the company. Prior to joining OPIX Technology, he was a lead quantitative researcher at American Century Investments. Previously, he served as a lead research analyst at Hudson River Trading, focusing on improving upon mathematical and statistical modelling, translate findings into algorithmic trading strategies in the market.

He received his bachelor’s degree in computer science from Georgia Institute of Technology.

CMO - James Rossi

James Rossi是OPIX Technology的CMO。他是一位營銷專家，專門從事品牌和客戶聯繫戰略、營銷技術解決方案和可持續業務增長。他擁有經濟學學士學位和超過20年的經驗，能夠為公司執行引人注目的定位。在此之前，他曾擔任美安公司的戰略營銷主管，負責確定有效的營銷方案和提升品牌形像以滿足公眾需求。

Our team loves to learn and keep up with the times and then to prepare for the future of the market. The atmosphere of learning and seizing opportunities gives us a great team cohesion.
